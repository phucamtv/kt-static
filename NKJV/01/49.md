<div class="title"><h1>49</h1><h3>Jacob’s Last Words to His Sons</h3></div>
<span class="verse sa_49_1"> <sup>1</sup>And Jacob called his sons and said, “Gather together, that I may <a data-toggle="tooltip" data-placement="bottom" title="Deut. 33:1, 6–25; (Amos 3:7)">⚓</a>tell you what shall befall you <a data-toggle="tooltip" data-placement="bottom" title="Num. 24:14; (Deut. 4:30); Is. 2:2; 39:6; Jer. 23:20; Heb. 1:2">⚓</a>in the last days:<br/></span>
<span class="verse sa_49_2">  <sup>2</sup>“Gather together and hear, you sons of Jacob,<br/>   And listen to Israel your father.<br/></span>
<span class="verse sa_49_3">  <sup>3</sup>“Reuben, you are <a data-toggle="tooltip" data-placement="bottom" title="Gen. 29:32">⚓</a>my firstborn,<br/>   My might and the beginning of my strength,<br/>   The excellency of dignity and the excellency of power.<br/></span>
<span class="verse sa_49_4">   <sup>4</sup>Unstable as water, you shall not excel,<br/>   Because you <a data-toggle="tooltip" data-placement="bottom" title="Gen. 35:22; Deut. 27:20; 1 Chr. 5:1">⚓</a>went up to your father’s bed;<br/>   Then you defiled <i>it</i>—<br/>   He went up to my couch.<br/></span>
<span class="verse sa_49_5">  <sup>5</sup>“Simeon and Levi <i>are</i> brothers;<br/>   Instruments of cruelty <i>are</i> <i>in</i> their dwelling place.<br/></span>
<span class="verse sa_49_6">   <sup>6</sup><a data-toggle="tooltip" data-placement="bottom" title="Ps. 64:2; Prov. 1:15, 16">⚓</a>Let not my soul enter their council;<br/>   Let not my honor be united <a data-toggle="tooltip" data-placement="bottom" title="Ps. 26:9; Eph. 5:11">⚓</a>to their assembly;<br/>   <a data-toggle="tooltip" data-placement="bottom" title="Gen. 34:26">⚓</a>For in their anger they slew a man,<br/>   And in their self-will they hamstrung an ox.<br/></span>
<span class="verse sa_49_7">   <sup>7</sup>Cursed <i>be</i> their anger, for <i>it</i> <i>is</i> fierce;<br/>   And their wrath, for it is cruel!<br/>   <a data-toggle="tooltip" data-placement="bottom" title="Num. 18:24; Josh. 19:1, 9; 21:1–42; 1 Chr. 4:24–27">⚓</a>I will divide them in Jacob<br/>   And scatter them in Israel.<br/></span>
<span class="verse sa_49_8">  <sup>8</sup>“Judah,<a data-toggle="tooltip" data-placement="bottom" title="Deut. 33:7; Rev. 5:5">⚓</a> you <i>are</i> <i>he</i> whom your brothers shall praise;<br/>   <a data-toggle="tooltip" data-placement="bottom" title="Ps. 18:40">⚓</a>Your hand <i>shall</i> <i>be</i> on the neck of your enemies;<br/>   <a data-toggle="tooltip" data-placement="bottom" title="Gen. 27:29; 1 Chr. 5:2">⚓</a>Your father’s children shall bow down before you.<br/></span>
<span class="verse sa_49_9">   <sup>9</sup>Judah <i>is</i><a data-toggle="tooltip" data-placement="bottom" title="Deut. 33:22; Ezek. 19:5–7; Mic. 5:8; (Rev. 5:5)">⚓</a>a lion’s whelp;<br/>   From the prey, my son, you have gone up.<br/>   <a data-toggle="tooltip" data-placement="bottom" title="Num. 23:24; 24:9">⚓</a>He bows down, he lies down as a lion;<br/>   And as a lion, who shall rouse him?<br/></span>
<span class="verse sa_49_10">   <sup>10</sup><a data-toggle="tooltip" data-placement="bottom" title="Num. 24:17; Jer. 30:21; Matt. 1:3; 2:6; Luke 3:33; Rev. 5:5">⚓</a>The scepter shall not depart from Judah,<br/>   Nor <a data-toggle="tooltip" data-placement="bottom" title="Ps. 60:7">⚓</a>a lawgiver from between his feet,<br/>   <a data-toggle="tooltip" data-placement="bottom" title="Is. 11:1; (Matt. 21:9)">⚓</a>Until Shiloh comes;<br/>   <a data-toggle="tooltip" data-placement="bottom" title="Deut. 18:15; Ps. 2:6–9; 72:8–11; Is. 42:1, 4; 49:6; 60:1–5; (Luke 2:30–32)">⚓</a>And to Him <i>shall</i> <i>be</i> the obedience of the people.<br/></span>
<span class="verse sa_49_11">   <sup>11</sup>Binding his donkey to the vine,<br/>   And his donkey’s colt to the choice vine,<br/>   He washed his garments in wine,<br/>   And his clothes in the blood of grapes.<br/></span>
<span class="verse sa_49_12">   <sup>12</sup>His eyes <i>are</i> darker than wine,<br/>   And his teeth whiter than milk.<br/></span>
<span class="verse sa_49_13">  <sup>13</sup>“Zebulun<a data-toggle="tooltip" data-placement="bottom" title="Deut. 33:18, 19; Josh. 19:10, 11">⚓</a> shall dwell by the haven of the sea;<br/>   He <i>shall</i> <i>become</i> a haven for ships,<br/>   And his border shall <a data-toggle="tooltip" data-placement="bottom" title="Gen. 10:19; Josh. 11:8">⚓</a>adjoin Sidon.<br/></span>
<span class="verse sa_49_14">  <sup>14</sup>“Issachar<a data-toggle="tooltip" data-placement="bottom" title="1 Chr. 12:32">⚓</a> is a strong donkey,<br/>   Lying down between two burdens;<br/></span>
<span class="verse sa_49_15">   <sup>15</sup>He saw that rest <i>was</i> good,<br/>   And that the land <i>was</i> pleasant;<br/>   He bowed <a data-toggle="tooltip" data-placement="bottom" title="1 Sam. 10:9">⚓</a>his shoulder to bear <i>a</i> <i>burden,</i><br/>   And became a band of slaves.<br/></span>
<span class="verse sa_49_16">  <sup>16</sup>“Dan<a data-toggle="tooltip" data-placement="bottom" title="Gen. 30:6; Deut. 33:22; Judg. 18:26, 27">⚓</a> shall judge his people<br/>   As one of the tribes of Israel.<br/></span>
<span class="verse sa_49_17">   <sup>17</sup><a data-toggle="tooltip" data-placement="bottom" title="Judg. 18:27">⚓</a>Dan shall be a serpent by the way,<br/>   A viper by the path,<br/>   That bites the horse’s heels<br/>   So that its rider shall fall backward.<br/></span>
<span class="verse sa_49_18">   <sup>18</sup><a data-toggle="tooltip" data-placement="bottom" title="Ex. 15:2; Ps. 25:5; 40:1–3; 119:166, 174; Is. 25:9; Mic. 7:7">⚓</a>I have waited for your salvation, O Lord!<br/></span>
<span class="verse sa_49_19">  <sup>19</sup>“Gad,<a data-toggle="tooltip" data-placement="bottom" title="Gen. 30:11; Deut. 33:20; 1 Chr. 5:18">⚓</a> a troop shall tramp upon him,<br/>   But he shall triumph at last.<br/></span>
<span class="verse sa_49_20">  <sup>20</sup>“Bread from <a data-toggle="tooltip" data-placement="bottom" title="Deut. 33:24; Josh. 19:24–31">⚓</a>Asher <i>shall</i> <i>be</i> rich,<br/>   And he shall yield royal dainties.<br/></span>
<span class="verse sa_49_21">  <sup>21</sup>“Naphtali<a data-toggle="tooltip" data-placement="bottom" title="Deut. 33:23">⚓</a><i>is</i> a deer let loose;<br/>   He uses beautiful words.<br/></span>
<span class="verse sa_49_22">  <sup>22</sup>“Joseph <i>is</i> a fruitful bough,<br/>   A fruitful bough by a well;<br/>   His branches run over the wall.<br/></span>
<span class="verse sa_49_23">   <sup>23</sup>The archers have <a data-toggle="tooltip" data-placement="bottom" title="Gen. 37:4, 24; Ps. 118:13">⚓</a>bitterly grieved him,<br/>   Shot <i>at</i> <i>him</i> and hated him.<br/></span>
<span class="verse sa_49_24">   <sup>24</sup>But his <a data-toggle="tooltip" data-placement="bottom" title="Job 29:20; Ps. 37:15">⚓</a>bow remained in strength,<br/>   And the arms of his hands were made strong<br/>   By the hands of <a data-toggle="tooltip" data-placement="bottom" title="Ps. 132:2, 5; Is. 1:24; 49:26">⚓</a>the Mighty <i>God</i> of Jacob<br/>   <a data-toggle="tooltip" data-placement="bottom" title="Gen. 45:11; 47:12">⚓</a>(From there <a data-toggle="tooltip" data-placement="bottom" title="(Ps. 23:1; 80:1)">⚓</a><i>is</i> the Shepherd, <a data-toggle="tooltip" data-placement="bottom" title="(Ps. 118:22); Is. 28:16; (1 Pet. 2:6–8)">⚓</a>the Stone of Israel),<br/></span>
<span class="verse sa_49_25">   <sup>25</sup><a data-toggle="tooltip" data-placement="bottom" title="Gen. 28:13; 32:9; 35:3; 43:23; 50:17">⚓</a>By the God of your father who will help you,<br/>   <a data-toggle="tooltip" data-placement="bottom" title="Gen. 17:1; 35:11">⚓</a>And by the Almighty <a data-toggle="tooltip" data-placement="bottom" title="Deut. 33:13">⚓</a>who will bless you<br/>   <i>With</i> blessings of heaven above,<br/>   Blessings of the deep that lies beneath,<br/>   Blessings of the breasts and of the womb.<br/></span>
<span class="verse sa_49_26">   <sup>26</sup>The blessings of your father<br/>   Have excelled the blessings of my ancestors,<br/>   <a data-toggle="tooltip" data-placement="bottom" title="Deut. 33:15; Hab. 3:6">⚓</a>Up to the utmost bound of the everlasting hills.<br/>   <a data-toggle="tooltip" data-placement="bottom" title="Deut. 33:16">⚓</a>They shall be on the head of Joseph,<br/>   And on the crown of the head of him who was separate from his brothers.<br/></span>
<span class="verse sa_49_27">  <sup>27</sup>“Benjamin is a <a data-toggle="tooltip" data-placement="bottom" title="Judg. 20:21, 25">⚓</a>ravenous wolf;<br/>   In the morning he shall devour the prey,<br/>   <a data-toggle="tooltip" data-placement="bottom" title="Num. 23:24; Esth. 8:11; Ezek. 39:10; Zech. 14:1">⚓</a>And at night he shall divide the spoil.”<br/></span>
<span class="verse sa_49_28"> <sup>28</sup>All these <i>are</i> the twelve tribes of Israel, and this <i>is</i> what their father spoke to them. And he blessed them; he blessed each one according to his own blessing.<br/></span>
<div class="title"><h3>Jacob’s Death and Burial</h3></div>
<span class="verse sa_49_29"> <sup>29</sup>Then he charged them and said to them: “I <a data-toggle="tooltip" data-placement="bottom" title="Gen. 15:15; 25:8; 35:29">⚓</a>am to be gathered to my people; <a data-toggle="tooltip" data-placement="bottom" title="Gen. 47:30; 2 Sam. 19:37">⚓</a>bury me with my fathers <a data-toggle="tooltip" data-placement="bottom" title="Gen. 23:16–20; 50:13">⚓</a>in the cave that <i>is</i> in the field of Ephron the Hittite, </span>
<span class="verse sa_49_30"><sup>30</sup>in the cave that <i>is</i> in the field of Machpelah, which <i>is</i> before Mamre in the land of Canaan, <a data-toggle="tooltip" data-placement="bottom" title="Gen. 23:3–20">⚓</a>which Abraham bought with the field of Ephron the Hittite as a possession for a burial place. </span>
<span class="verse sa_49_31"><sup>31</sup><a data-toggle="tooltip" data-placement="bottom" title="Gen. 23:19, 20; 25:9">⚓</a>There they buried Abraham and Sarah his wife, <a data-toggle="tooltip" data-placement="bottom" title="Gen. 35:29; 50:13">⚓</a>there they buried Isaac and Rebekah his wife, and there I buried Leah. </span>
<span class="verse sa_49_32"><sup>32</sup>The field and the cave that <i>is</i> there <i>were</i> purchased from the sons of Heth.” </span>
<span class="verse sa_49_33"><sup>33</sup>And when Jacob had finished commanding his sons, he drew his feet up into the bed and breathed his last, and was gathered to his people.<br/></span>
