<div class="title"><h1>94</h1><h3>God the Refuge of the Righteous</h3></div>
<span class="verse thi_94_1">  <sup>1</sup>O Lord God, <a data-toggle="tooltip" data-placement="bottom" title="Deut. 32:35; (Is. 35:4; Nah. 1:2; Rom. 12:19)">⚓</a>to whom vengeance belongs—<br/>   O God, to whom vengeance belongs, shine forth!<br/></span>
<span class="verse thi_94_2">   <sup>2</sup>Rise up, O <a data-toggle="tooltip" data-placement="bottom" title="(Gen. 18:25)">⚓</a>Judge of the earth;<br/>   Render punishment to the proud.<br/></span>
<span class="verse thi_94_3">   <sup>3</sup>Lord, <a data-toggle="tooltip" data-placement="bottom" title="(Job 20:5)">⚓</a>how long will the wicked,<br/>   How long will the wicked triumph?<br/></span>
<span class="verse thi_94_4">  <sup>4</sup>They <a data-toggle="tooltip" data-placement="bottom" title="Ps. 31:18; Jude 15">⚓</a>utter speech, <i>and</i> speak insolent things;<br/>   All the workers of iniquity boast in themselves.<br/></span>
<span class="verse thi_94_5">   <sup>5</sup>They break in pieces Your people, O Lord,<br/>   And afflict Your heritage.<br/></span>
<span class="verse thi_94_6">   <sup>6</sup>They slay the widow and the stranger,<br/>   And murder the fatherless.<br/></span>
<span class="verse thi_94_7">   <sup>7</sup><a data-toggle="tooltip" data-placement="bottom" title="Job 22:13; Ps. 10:11">⚓</a>Yet they say, “The Lord does not see,<br/>   Nor does the God of Jacob understand.”<br/></span>
<span class="verse thi_94_8">  <sup>8</sup>Understand, you senseless among the people;<br/>   And <i>you</i> fools, when will you be wise?<br/></span>
<span class="verse thi_94_9">   <sup>9</sup><a data-toggle="tooltip" data-placement="bottom" title="(Ex. 4:11; Prov. 20:12)">⚓</a>He who planted the ear, shall He not hear?<br/>   He who formed the eye, shall He not see?<br/></span>
<span class="verse thi_94_10">   <sup>10</sup>He who instructs the nations, shall He not correct,<br/>   He who teaches man knowledge?<br/></span>
<span class="verse thi_94_11">   <sup>11</sup>The Lord<a data-toggle="tooltip" data-placement="bottom" title="Job 11:11; 1 Cor. 3:20">⚓</a>knows the thoughts of man,<br/>   That they <i>are</i> futile.<br/></span>
<span class="verse thi_94_12">  <sup>12</sup>Blessed <i>is</i> the man whom You <a data-toggle="tooltip" data-placement="bottom" title="(Deut. 8:5; Job 5:17; Ps. 119:71; Prov. 3:11, 12; Heb. 12:5, 6)">⚓</a>instruct, O Lord,<br/>   And teach out of Your law,<br/></span>
<span class="verse thi_94_13">   <sup>13</sup>That You may give him rest from the days of adversity,<br/>   Until the pit is dug for the wicked.<br/></span>
<span class="verse thi_94_14">   <sup>14</sup>For the Lord will not cast off His people,<br/>   Nor will He forsake His inheritance.<br/></span>
<span class="verse thi_94_15">   <sup>15</sup>But judgment will return to righteousness,<br/>   And all the upright in heart will follow it.<br/></span>
<span class="verse thi_94_16">  <sup>16</sup>Who will rise up for me against the evildoers?<br/>   Who will stand up for me against the workers of iniquity?<br/></span>
<span class="verse thi_94_17">   <sup>17</sup>Unless the Lord<i>had</i> <i>been</i> my help,<br/>   My soul would soon have settled in silence.<br/></span>
<span class="verse thi_94_18">   <sup>18</sup>If I say, “My foot slips,”<br/>   Your mercy, O Lord, will hold me up.<br/></span>
<span class="verse thi_94_19">   <sup>19</sup>In the multitude of my anxieties within me,<br/>   Your comforts delight my soul.<br/></span>
<span class="verse thi_94_20">  <sup>20</sup>Shall <a data-toggle="tooltip" data-placement="bottom" title="Amos 6:3">⚓</a>the throne of iniquity, which devises evil by law,<br/>   Have fellowship with You?<br/></span>
<span class="verse thi_94_21">   <sup>21</sup>They gather together against the life of the righteous,<br/>   And condemn <a data-toggle="tooltip" data-placement="bottom" title="(Ex. 23:7); Ps. 106:38; (Prov. 17:15); Matt. 27:4">⚓</a>innocent blood.<br/></span>
<span class="verse thi_94_22">   <sup>22</sup>But the Lord has been my defense,<br/>   And my God the rock of my refuge.<br/></span>
<span class="verse thi_94_23">   <sup>23</sup>He has brought on them their own iniquity,<br/>   And shall cut them off in their own wickedness;<br/>   The Lord our God shall cut them off.<br/></span>
